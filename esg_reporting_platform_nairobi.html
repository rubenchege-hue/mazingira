🌍 Kenya Climate Risk Context
Nairobi faces physical risks: increased flooding (Nairobi River basin), prolonged dry spells affecting water supply. Transition risks: Kenya's National Climate Change Action Plan (NCCAP 2018–2022) and Energy Act 2019 will drive decarbonisation requirements. East African Rift geothermal opportunity is a key resilience lever.
